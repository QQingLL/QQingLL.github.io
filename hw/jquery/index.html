Today, I will teach you how to make Braised Pork, which is one of my favorite Chinese food. Even I do not like meat, I can not resist its charm. This is a intro course to Chinese food, and the secret about Chinese food is Seasoning. If you follow my steps, you will sucess!

Braised Pork

First, you need to prepare a purple onion. Then cut it into small pieces. Add oil into the pot. Start fried onion with low heat until onion become gold color.

Add sliced pork into the hot onion oil! Stir Fry until the pork become golden color.

Now, it is the time for our magic Seasoning to work.

Add
  • 2 spoon soy sauce
  • 2 spoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 spoon suger
  • 1 piece star anise
  • 1 piece bay leaf
  • 1 piece cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Last step: Add Enough Water

Add boiled eggs. If you do not like egg, you can ignore this step

1.5 hours waiting! The time will make the pork meat melted in your mouth.
